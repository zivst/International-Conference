Venue

The Conference will be held in Bar-Ilan University, Ramat-Gan.

Ramat-Gan (Hebrew: רמת-גן) is a city in the Tel Aviv District of Israel, located east of the municipality of Tel Aviv and part of the Tel Aviv metropolitan areal.

In order to get from the Ben Gurion Airport to Bar-Ilan University, take a train to Tel-Aviv HaShalom. From the station one can reach the University by bus (line 56, 64, 116, 364, 472 or 473).

For those who arrives by cars please click here for directions.


Kfar Maccabiah Hotel is located in 3km from Bar-Ilan university. The organizers will provide a transportation from\ to the hotel.
